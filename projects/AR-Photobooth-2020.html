AR Photobooth (2020)

AR Photobooth is a prototype augmented reality photobooth that takes pictures of participants with 3D model filters on them. As the participant get close to the camera, the photobooth automatically starts the camera taking sequence. Different 3D model filters are cycle based on the number particpants on the screen. Participants have 5 seconds to pose before it automatically takes the picture. Once the picture is taken, participants can scan a QR code from the screen to download the photo from the server.
