Bahadur (2017)

Bahadur is a 2D top-down shooter game that take place in an Arabic theme dungeon. Player plays as Bahadur, who are known to be a powerful spellcaster strip from his power and fell down deep into the deepest dungeon. With the help of a mysterious djinn, he became stronger once again as he goes up the floors. Player gain four elemental powers that each has strength and weakness against the elemental golems and monsters appear within the dungeon and with each mini boss destroyed, an upgrade skill is given for the player to improve Bahadur in health, attack, and speed.

Role: Gameplay Programmer, UI Programmer, UI Artist

For: Year 2 – Game Project Studio 1 (Bachelor Degree of Game Development, KDU University)

Platform: Windows PC

Engine: Unity 3D (C#)

What I’ve worked on:

  • Tutorial sequence on programming and design with dialogue prompts that teaches the gameplay to the player.
  • User Interface for the Main menu, Upgrade Menu, Gameplay HUD and Cursor.
  • VFX particles and lighting on the torches and the atmosphere of the game.
