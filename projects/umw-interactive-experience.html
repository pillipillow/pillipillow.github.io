UMW Interactive Experience (2023)

UMW Interactive Experience is a showcase experience for UMW Holding's industrial and marine equipment. The experience is shown on two screens. The right view presented on a projector screen, shows the 3D overview of areas and equipments in action. The left view presented on a touch screen allow users to explore through the equipment catalog in detail; each with the equipments's name, description and their manufacturer's logo.
