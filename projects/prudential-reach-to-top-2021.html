Prudential Powerup to ToT (2021)

Prudential Powerup to ToT is a 2D endless running game, where player climb as high as possible to get the highest score. Player have to avoid randomise obstacles and meteors to keep going, while choosing to collect orbs that multiply the score along the way. At each 50 points, player can reach a different rank where the obstacles get frequent and the player climbing speed get faster as difficulty gets harder.
